✨ Features

🚀

Auto-Scroll

Automatically scrolls through your entire conversation history, loading older messages as it goes.

👥

Both Participants

Extracts messages from both you and the other person with clear sender identification.

📅

Date Headers

Includes timestamps and date headers to organize messages chronologically.

📁

Clean Export

Downloads as readable text files with proper formatting and UTF-8 encoding.

🛡️

Privacy First

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data sent to external servers.

⚡

High Performance

Optimized for large conversations with memory management and batch processing.

🔧 How It Works

1

Install Extension

Download and install the Chrome extension from our download page.

2

Open Messenger

Go to Facebook Messenger and open any conversation you want to export.

3

Click Export

Click the extension icon and hit "Start Export" to begin the automatic process.

4

Get Your File

Wait for the export to complete and download your conversation as a text file.
